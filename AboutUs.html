About the Company

MicroTronic PCB Fab Solution is a distinguished consulting firm and a leading contract manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of specialized, high-precision, high-reliability, and high-density Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs). Committed to excellence, we collaborate with ISO 9001:2015-certified entities to uphold international standards in PCB design and manufacturing.

Our comprehensive, end-to-end services cover the entire PCB lifecycle—from layout and design to fabrication—providing a seamless, one-stop solution tailored to our clients' needs. With operations spanning the USA and India, we leverage decades of collective expertise to empower manufacturers and exporters with cutting-edge PCB solutions.
